Diet and Nutrition Tips

There are several things we need to take into consideration if we are thinking of the best diet for a woman. The food choices can help reduce PMS, boost fertility, combat stress, make pregnancy easier, and ease the symptoms of menopause.

Good Nutrition for Women

Good nutrition starts with the basics: a well-rounded diet consisting of whole grains, fresh fruits and vegetables, healthy fats, and lean sources of protein.

   Focus on whole, plant-based foods.

   Eat to control carvings and boost energy.

   Get plenty of good carbohydrates.

   Maintain Healthy Fat.

   Focus on Food for Strong Bones.

   Bone up on calcium.

   Don’t eat too much protein.

   Make sure you get enough iron.

   Cut back on caffeine.

Nutrition tips to ease the Symptoms of PMS:

   Avoid trans fats, refined sugar, and salt.

   Cut out caffeine and alcohol.

   Limit red meat and egg yolks.

   Try cutting out dairy.

   Add essential fatty acids to the diet.

   Consider vitamin supplements.

Nutrition Tips for Healthy Pregnancy and for breastfeeding:

   Focus on a balanced diet with sufficient fat and proteins.

   Cut down caffeine consumption.

   Eat smaller and more frequent meals. 

   Need to be cautious about foods that may be harmful to pregnant women.

   Keep your caloric consumption a little higher

   Emphasize lean sources of protein and calcium

   Take prenatal vitamin supplements

Nutrition tips for Menopause:

   Boost calcium intake.

   Limit wine, sugar, white flour products, and coffee.

   Eat more good fats. 

   Try flaxseed for hot flashes.

   Consider eating more soy
